# AI Service Desk Product System

Enterprise service desk UI for support and IT analysts. Product register, high information density, restrained color, clear status semantics, and visible AI provenance.

## Design dials

- Visual variance: 5
- Motion intensity: 3
- Information density: 8

## Layout

Use a persistent left sidebar, dense metric strips, structured lists, side panels for agent activity, and form flows with clear labels. Avoid marketing hero composition.

## Components

Ticket queue rows, status badges, priority indicators, agent timeline, retrieval source list, audit trail, intake form, policy chips, and action toolbar.

## Interaction

Every long AI action shows current step, source evidence, confidence, and human override affordances. Errors stay inline near affected content.
