# ADR 001: Extract Ticket Workspace View From App

## Status

Accepted

## Context

`App.tsx` should orchestrate authenticated application state, but it still embeds the full ticket treatment workspace. The workspace is a coherent ticket feature surface and already has related modules under `views/tickets`.

## Decision

Move `TicketWorkspaceView` to `frontend/src/views/tickets/TicketWorkspaceView.tsx` and keep `App.tsx` as the caller that supplies data and mutation callbacks.

## Consequences

- Future ticket workspace changes can happen in a focused module.
- `App.tsx` becomes smaller and easier to reason about.
- The view keeps its current behavior and avoids a visual rewrite during architectural cleanup.
