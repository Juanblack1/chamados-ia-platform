# ADR-001: Extract cohesive workspace panels before moving the whole workspace

## Status

Accepted

## Context

The full ticket workspace still contains mutation wiring and several presentation panels. Moving the entire workspace at once would increase risk.

## Decision

First extract the cohesive presentation panels and helpers into `TicketWorkspacePanels.tsx`, leaving the workspace container in `App.tsx`.

## Consequences

- `App.tsx` becomes smaller with lower-risk movement.
- Ticket support panels become easier to test and evolve.
- A future full workspace extraction has fewer dependencies.
